SUSTAINABILITY

GO-GREEN INITIATIVES

  1. Investing in latest & most sustainable technology which is LESS-water, chemicals, energy and discharge in Washing & Dry Process with Laser Technology to produce 120,000 units a day, expected to start in Q2 2020.
  2. Use of certified green chemicals and ZHDC certified chemicals
  3. Installing Effluent Treatment Plant by Q2 2020 with capacity of 2000000 liters/day & recycle it.
  4. Installing 1.8 Mega Watts of Solar power to make it Green manufacturing, this is equivalent to saving 33000 tons of carbon footprint & 65000 trees in its lifetime.
  5. Increased use of Recycled raw material.

CSR to Uplift Community

  1. Development
    1. Free vocational training of skill development to community.
    2. Support women empowerment & youth to prepare them to take a first job or be self employed or be a SME.
    3. Providing opportunities for people living with physical challenges and giving second chance to ex-convicts.

  1. Security
    1. No one will sleep hungry policy” by providing vouchers for monthly grocery.

  1. Medical & Health Benefits
    1. Arranging medical and life insurance for our workers.
    2. We run health awareness programs such as HIV Sensitization / Support early detection tests for cervical & breast cancer / EYE testing / Gynecological testing.

  1. Support
    1. Supporting school, Orphanage & Old Age homes in our neighborhood.
    2. Extending sponsorship to under privileged bright students in our community.
    3. Supporting football team of bright players from out worker, who have already won 2nd division league and are part of national super league now.

  1. Networking and Collaboration

    2. We take special interest in networking and collaborating with other govt and non-govt organisations those who deals with and enhances the wellbeing of the community around us. Like :

    1. St. Joseph Catholic Church Mulolongo – offering technical training to local unemployed youth
    2. Shadrack Juma Foundation Baba Dogo – training women & Single mothers with art of garment making.
    3. Kenya women Children Wellness Centre – implementation of progs to eliminate gender-based violence, eliminate cultural barriers that prevents women from financial liberation through capacity building on entrepreneurship skills
