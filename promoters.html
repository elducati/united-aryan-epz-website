Enterprises Managed by Our Promoters

Pan Global Merchants, Abidjan

The company has been in operations for the last 19 years and has steadily grown year after year. The company has a Supply Chain Management system linked to a Customer Relationship Management system for better servicing of the international and domestic customers while maintaining a high degree of profitability to the core investor group. The company has a fully integrated unit employing 380 persons. It also has in-house purchasers of paddy and rice who are supported with a team of laboratory technicians and milling technicians working as a team to offer the highest degree of quality and satisfaction The company operates a state of the art SATAKE Rice Milling plant and has recently commissioned a second SATAKE high-speed line. The company is ISO 9002 certified in addition to being certified by the Food and Drug Administration (FDA). The company services customers across nineteen countries: Australia, Belgium, Canada, France, Germany, Italy, Kuwait, Mauritius, Norway, Oman, Poland, Qatar, Saudi Arabia, South Africa, Sweden, Switzerland, United Arab Emirates, United Kingdom, United States of America. The annual turnover of the company for the year ended March 2002 is approximately U$ 8.53 million.

Crowne Plaza Surya, New Delhi, India

In the heart of New Friends Colony, New Delhi stands one of the most prestigious addresses, the Suryaa, a five-star deluxe hotel built in 1982 offering luxuriously appointed 230 guest rooms and suites, providing elegance and comfort to make the stay truly memorable. The multi-cuisine restaurants and bar feature varied culinary experiences providing a wide selection of food and beverage facilities, and offer all-day dining, plus 24-hour Room Service and a discotheque. Conference and banqueting facilities are suitable for any occasion, with the additional services of a Business Centre. Extensive fitness facilities include an open-air swimming pool and a fully equipped gymnasium for guests' relaxation and exercise. This hotel, which stands for true excellence in courtesy and hospitality, is also conveniently located close to Delhi's prime commercial center and is an ideal base from which to explore the tourist and historical attractions of the city.

Growmore General Trading, Kuwait

After the Kuwait invasion in 1991, the promoters of Al-Zahem & Malhotra saw the opportunity for security services in Kuwait and entered into a joint venture with the international chain of Security providers GROUP 4 Securites. The company specializes in the installation of security systems and guarding services. To its credit, the company has the accounts of the 7 out of the 9 banks in Kuwait and is today a leading player in the field of security services. In 2001, the company has achieved a turnover of US$3.6 million.

SIDI

SIDI is a private limited company incorporated in Cote d ’ Ivoire , whose principal objective is to design and develop a socially responsible residential/commercial scheme, a new town, in the province of Abidjan, Ivory Coast ’ s most populace city. SIDI through its subsidiaries own development land in India; United Arab Emirates & Africa. It is investing in land Banking, endeavoring to build self-contained townships. Le Reve is a development of c.6,000 residential units, x3 hospitals, x9 schools and 116 acres dedicated to commercial use on a 292 hectares site located 20 minutes’ drive from Abidjan ’ s International Airport and 800 meters from the picturesque coastline. The development abuts the main coastal highway into Abidjan , the world ’ s third largest French speaking city with c/ 5m residents. SIDI has established a team of professionals to handle all aspects of the development from master planning, road planning, architects, civil engineers, sewage/ Drainage planners, electricity planners, Water planners & interior designers. In 2012;SIDI is going to Launch “Le Rêve” in Grand Bassam - Ivory Coast.
