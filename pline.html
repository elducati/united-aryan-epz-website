Production Line

The company started with 1 unit of 17 lines, and in mid 2006 capacity was enhanced to 38 lines when the 2nd unit was taken up. Currently our facility set up spread within 3 Units & the 4th Unit is under development which will be starting its operations soon. all the units are located in same compound of Balaji Export Processing Zone. Zone located in the heart of the city and close distance from major hotels and city centre.
